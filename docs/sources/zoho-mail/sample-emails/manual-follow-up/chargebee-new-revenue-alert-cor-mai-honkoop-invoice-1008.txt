File purpose:
This file is a cleaned plain-text sample of a Chargebee New Revenue Alert email.
It is intended for workflow discovery and dashboard monitoring rules.

Chargebee notification email

Dashboard category:
Manual Follow-up

Suggested event type:
chargebee_new_revenue_alert

Suggested severity:
orange

Reason:
This email indicates that Chargebee generated the first invoice for a customer or subscription. It may require follow-up verification, such as checking whether the customer/subscription is mapped to the correct workflow, whether tree allocation is expected, whether a welcome or thank-you email was sent, and whether the subscription is visible in the monitoring registry.

Extracted fields:
- Email subject: New Revenue Alert
- Alert type: First invoice generated
- Customer name: Cor & Mai Honkoop
- Customer email: corenmai@hotmail.com
- Customer/subscription reference: 199Hc7V4uAEO2WBD
- Plan/Add-on: 1-boom-per-maand
- Amount: € 10,00
- Invoice number: 1008
- Source system: Chargebee

Expected follow-up:
- Verify whether the Chargebee plan `1-boom-per-maand` maps to an active subscription workflow.
- Verify whether tree allocation is expected for this plan.
- Verify whether the customer exists in PostgreSQL `users1` or the relevant CRM/contact system.
- Verify whether a tree allocation event exists, if applicable.
- Verify whether an outbound welcome/thank-you email was sent.
- Verify whether this invoice appears in the Chargebee invoice export.
- Keep this as a manual follow-up candidate until the Chargebee workflow is mapped and audited.

Suggested dashboard rule:
- Green: invoice is matched with a known Chargebee plan workflow, expected tree/customer/email actions are complete, and outbound evidence exists where required.
- Orange: invoice email exists but workflow mapping, tree allocation, customer registration, or outbound email evidence is missing or unknown.
- Red: invoice processing failed, subscription workflow failed, tree allocation failed, or follow-up remains open beyond the agreed SLA.
